Goli Baje

Are soft and spongy deep-fried fritters made with rice flour, and chickpea flour. Served as popular snack in costal cuisine as tea time snack.

Kori rotti

A spicy dish of Tulu Udupi-Mangalorean cuisine, a combination of red-chili and coconut milk based chicken curry and crisp, dry wafers made from boiled rice.

Mangalore buns

Sweet fried bread made with mashed bananas, flour, sugar and spiced with a touch of ground cumin. These sweet banana buns as they are known is a specialty dish from the Mangalore region.
